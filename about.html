About Salvation of Souls MC

Club History

Founded: November 24th, 2024

Salvation of Souls MC was born in the fire of the outlaw world, originally established as a 1%er motorcycle club led by its founding trio: John-Frank Fury, Nova Rayne-Fury, and Luna Rayne. Built on loyalty, grit, and an unbreakable bond, the club quickly found its footing and carved out a presence in El Burro Heights, where its first clubhouse stood as a symbol of brotherhood and power.

For a time, the club thrived. The engines roared, the patch meant something, and the family stood strong. But as time passed, life outside the club began to pull members in different directions. Activity slowed, numbers thinned, and eventually, Salvation of Souls MC was respectfully retired-its legacy left dormant, but never forgotten.

That silence didn’t last forever.

On November 24th, 2024, Luna Rayne approached John-Frank Fury with a vision-not to rebuild what once was, but to evolve it. The idea was simple: bring Salvation of Souls MC back, not as a 1%er, but as a 99%er club focused on redemption, growth, and unity. John-Frank Fury gave his blessing, and with that, a new era began.

The rebirth of the club was led by Luna Rayne, Trey Rayne, and Jessi Rayne, forming the foundation of the Redemption-era leadership. Establishing themselves in Star City, the club began to grow at a remarkable pace. This new chapter wasn’t about outlaw notoriety-it was about earning respect through action.

Salvation of Souls MC became known for its presence in the community. Members put in work across the city-gathering materials, hunting, fishing, supporting local businesses-doing whatever was needed to lend a hand and build something meaningful. The club wasn’t just riding together-they were building something bigger than themselves.

A major milestone came with the club’s first World Ride, celebrating one full year since its rebirth. It marked not just survival, but success.

But growth brings its own challenges.

In the months that followed, activity in Star City began to decline. Recognizing the need to adapt, Luna Rayne, Trey Rayne, and Jessi Rayne called a full club meeting. With strong numbers still in their ranks, a new idea was brought to the table: expansion through division.

A vote was held-and passed unanimously.

Salvation of Souls MC would split into two distinct chapters:

  • Redemption Chapter - a 99%er chapter rooted in the club’s reformed ideals
  • Tarnished Chapter - a return to the club’s 1%er origins

With the restructuring came the formation of International leadership:

  • Luna Rayne - International President
  • Trey Rayne - International Vice President
  • Jessi Rayne - International Sergeant at Arms

Leadership within the chapters was established soon after.

The Redemption Chapter remained true to its 99%er identity, with Xena Mironova voted in as President-tasked with continuing the club’s legacy of community, structure, and respect.

Meanwhile, the Tarnished Chapter took root in Truepath City, embracing the outlaw path once more. Led by Luna Rayne, Trey Rayne, and Jessi Rayne, this chapter represented the darker, original spirit of the club. Its founding members-Nova Rayne-Fury, Wyatt Turner, Renee Riley, and Clyde Riley-cemented its place as a force to be reckoned with.

Now, Salvation of Souls MC stands divided-but stronger than ever. Two paths. Two identities. One patch.

The road ahead is uncertain, but one thing is guaranteed-

This story is far from over.
